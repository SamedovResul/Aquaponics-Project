Sustainable Agriculture

The Problem

  • Globally increasing food demand.
  • Scarcity of water and available land for agriculture.
  • Climate change and unpredictable weather conditions.
  • Crop losses caused by invasive species and pests.

The Solution

Self Balancing System

The harmful fish waste is nitrated with organic chemicals, turning it into a necessary food for the plant and after the plant roots clean the water it is transferred back to the fish tank.

Value Proposition

Enclosed smart farming area that removes dependence on fertilisers

Crop and meat production at the same time

IoT Integrated and Automated System
